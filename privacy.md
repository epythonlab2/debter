Privacy Policy for debter (ደብተር)

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

At debter (ደብተር), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the financial and business data you trust us with. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information based on your account role.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional, secure, and cloud-synced daily sales ledger for micro-shops, we collect the following information:

Account Identifiers: We collect phone numbers as unique account identifiers for Shop Owners and Salespersons to securely verify identity, log access, and sync data. Authentication is securely handled through our backend database infrastructure.

Business, Inventory & Sales Data: We store custom inventory templates (item names and default prices), transaction logs (prices, quantities, and dates), and salesperson assignments to generate your dashboard statistics and digital ledger.

Device Permissions: Our app only requests Internet Access permissions to communicate with our cloud servers to sync your data. We do not access your contacts, camera, photos, calendar, or GPS location.

2. Role-Based Data Access and Permissions

To protect the confidentiality of your shop's financial records, data access is segmented by system roles:

Shop Owners: Have complete administrative access to their own shop's data. They can view all daily sales dashboards, check historical reports, and create and manage salesperson accounts.

Salespersons: Can record daily sales transactions, view sales logs for their assigned shifts, and register, update, or manage inventory items and templates. They cannot manage other user accounts or shop owner settings.

Super Administrator: The Super Admin has system-level oversight strictly limited to activating or deactivating Shop Owner accounts (e.g., for subscription or onboarding purposes). The Super Admin does not have access to, and cannot view, edit, manage, or audit any shop's sales records, transactional logs, or inventory catalogs. Your financial data remains private to your business.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

Identify, secure, and maintain your personal merchant or salesperson account.

Sync your ledger entries in real-time across authorized devices.

Calculate daily revenue, averages, and display visual analytics for the shop owner.

Provide account verification and support.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your business sales logs, phone numbers, or personal information with any third-party advertisers or external organizations.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is synced in real-time and stored securely on cloud database servers managed by our service infrastructure provider, Supabase. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to secure data in transit and at rest.

Please note that while we take extreme care to safeguard your business information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Control and Permanent Record Rules (Your Rights)

Permanent Ledger Integrity (No Deletion): To preserve the historical accuracy, financial integrity, and auditability of your business operations, individual sales records cannot be deleted or modified once registered in the system. This prevents ledger tampering and ensures salesperson accountability.

Account Activation/Deactivation: Shop Owners can manage the active status of their salespersons. If a Shop Owner wishes to temporarily deactivate or permanently retire their entire shop ledger, they can request account deactivation by contacting our support line.

6. Children’s Privacy

debter (ደብተር) is a business utility and ledger tool designed for micro-shop owners and adult merchants. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has registered with their phone number, we will delete their account and data from our servers immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
